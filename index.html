Who is Cleanware?

Here at Cleanware, we have a mission to make software development more available to the masses.

With libraries like EasyLib and EasyJSON, C development has never been easier.




What we do

Cleanware was formed to make software development easier and more accessible, bringing down that barrier of entry for those

who may find it too hard or a bit daunting. Computer science is for anyone.
